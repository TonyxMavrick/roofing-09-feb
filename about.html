Our Story

Excellence in Roofing Since Day One

RockSafe Roofing was founded on a simple principle: deliver exceptional roofing services with uncompromising quality and integrity. What started as a small team of dedicated roofing professionals has grown into one of New York's most trusted roofing companies.

Over the years, we've completed thousands of roofing projects for homeowners, property managers, and commercial building owners throughout the New York metropolitan area. Our reputation is built on quality workmanship, honest communication, and standing behind every job we complete.

Today, RockSafe Roofing continues to set the standard for excellence in the roofing industry. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern materials and techniques to provide roofing solutions that protect your investment and exceed your expectations.

Why Choose Us

What Sets Us Apart

Licensed & Insured Professionals

All our roofing contractors are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We maintain comprehensive insurance coverage including general liability and workers' compensation to protect you and our team.

Our technicians receive ongoing training in the latest roofing techniques and safety protocols, ensuring every project meets the highest industry standards.

Premium Materials & Warranties

We partner with leading roofing material manufacturers to offer you the best products available. From architectural shingles to metal roofing systems, every material we use is backed by manufacturer warranties.

In addition to manufacturer warranties, we provide our own workmanship warranty on all installations, giving you complete peace of mind.

Transparent Pricing

We believe in honest, upfront pricing with no hidden fees. Every estimate we provide is detailed and transparent, clearly outlining materials, labor, and timeline.

Our free estimates come with no obligation, and we'll work within your budget to find the best roofing solution for your needs.

24/7 Emergency Service

Roofing emergencies don't wait for business hours. That's why we offer 24/7 emergency roofing services to address urgent situations like storm damage or severe leaks.
